Base Popover

Popovers can be presented in any direction you choose. Popover modifiers are named by the direction in which they'll appear. For example, a popover with .popover__bottom applied will appear below its originating element.

.s-popover__top

Popover appears centered above the trigger

.s-popover__top-left

Popover appears above and to the left of trigger

.s-popover__top-right

Popover appears above and to the right of trigger

.s-popover__bottom

Popover appears centered below the trigger

.s-popover__bottom-left

Popover appears below and to the left of trigger

.s-popover__bottom-right

Popover appears below and to the right of trigger

.s-popover__right

Popover appears to the right of the trigger centered vertically.

.s-popover__right-top

Popover appears to the right of the trigger aligned to the top vertically.

.s-popover__right-bottom

Popover appears to the right of the trigger aligned to the bottom vertically.

.s-popover__left

Popover appears to the left of the trigger centered vertically.

.s-popover__left-top

Popover appears to the left of the trigger aligned to the top vertically.

.s-popover__left-bottom

Popover appears to the left of the trigger aligned to the bottom vertically.
